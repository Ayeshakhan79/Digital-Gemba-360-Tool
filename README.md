# Digital-Gemba-360-Tool
This repository presents a **digital Gemba application** developed to replace paper-based processes and enhance accountability through structured observation logging, action tracking, and automated notifications.  

**Note:** All screenshots are blurred to maintain confidentiality.

---

## 1. Application Overview

The Gemba App enables teams to **perform structured Gemba walks**, capture observations directly from the shop floor, and track actions to closure.

It is designed to standardize the Gemba process, reduce manual documentation, and provide greater transparency into improvement activities.

### Key Capabilities
- Digital Gemba walk submission with standardized fields
- Capture of observations, evidence, and improvement ideas
- Assignment of actions to responsible users
- Real-time status visibility 
- Automated reminders and follow-ups
- Dashboard visualization of trends and performance

---

## 2. Improvements & Impact

- Reduced manual reporting effort by digitizing Gemba walk forms
- Improved visibility of open actions and overdue tasks
- Increased completion rate through automated reminders
- Clear ownership with assigned action leaders
- Trend analysis enabled through standardized data collection
- Improved accountability and follow-through
- Reduced process delays caused by email-based tracking
